Privacy



What is stored on BusChecker servers

Nothing.

What may be stored on 3rd-party servers

Your phone number, and any stop code and route number that is requested through the app.

What is stored on your phone

Any stop code, route number and name that you add. Any setting that you set in the app will be stored. Quick search won't store anything.

Clear data

If you uninstall BusChecker, all data, except the one stored on 3rd-party servers (transport companies) will be erased.
