Install



  • 1. Accept all of your browser's warnings.
  • 2. Open the file that was downloaded (BusChecker.apk).
  • 3. Click on Settings, and on Allow from this source.
  • 4. Click on Install.
  • 5. BusChecker is now installed on your phone!
